Our Solutions

Home Energy Storage System

The residential energy storage system uses low-cost electricity from rooftop solar power generation devices and social power supply systems to store excess electricity in the energy storage system, ensuring that users can use environmentally friendly energy all day long.

The system can not only serve as an emergency power source, but also effectively save household electricity expenses. It is suitable for general residential appliances, computers, lighting and communication equipment.

Industrial and Commercial Energy Storage

As the load capacity of industrial and commercial complexes continues to increase and the load peak-valley difference continues to widen, the requirements for power supply quality and stability are becoming higher.

Rollins Energy can flexibly match various industrial and commercial scenarios, and support multi-mode operation at the same time, which improves investment returns, achieves peak-valley time shifting, staggered power consumption, and relieves power grid pressure.

Intelligent Power Change System

Smart battery swapping is a way to solve users' charging problems and mileage anxiety by replacing "charging" with "replacement". The battery swapping battery adopts A-grade lithium iron phosphate cells and has an IP67 protection level.

The battery service provides electronic fences and GPS monitoring functions, which can monitor the real-time location and driving speed to provide data support for efficiency optimization.

E-Motorcycle Battery

Designed for two-/three-wheeler vehicles and high-speed e-motorcycles, featuring an IP67 protection rating for water and dust resistance, as well as high-temperature durability.

Built with laser welding and a rugged enclosure, the battery structure is stronger, more stable, and highly reliable. An intelligent battery management system integrates a high-accuracy SOC algorithm, temperature monitoring, and cell balancing.

Parking & Starting Battery

As demand for reliable parking power and cold-start performance continues to rise across construction machinery and logistics fleets, Rollins Energy is expanding its Parking Start Battery portfolio to meet the needs of real-world operations.

Backed by large-scale manufacturing capacity, intelligent production processes, and stringent quality control, our solutions are engineered for high safety, long service life, and strong low-temperature adaptability.
